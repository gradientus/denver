Denver, Colorado

The Mile High City

  • 1858 - Denver City was founded in the Kansas Territory.
  • 1861 - Denver City becomes part of Colorado Territory.
  • 1867 - Denver City changes names to Denver.  Denver becomes the capital of Colorado Territory.
  • 1876 - Colorado becomes a state. Denver becomes the Colorado state capital.
  • 1890 - Elitch Gardens amusement park opens.
  • 1894 - Denver State Capitol building opens.
  • 1896 - Denver Zooological Gardens opens.
  • 1908 - Colorado Museum of Natural History built.
  • 1910 - Daniels & Fisher Tower built.
  • 1911 - Cathedral Basilica of the Immaculate Conception built.
  • 1915 - Cheesman Park opens.
  • 1930 - Paramount Theater opens.
  • 1934 - Denver Symphony Orchestra established.
  • 1951 - Denver Coliseum opens.
  • 1960 - Denver Broncos active.
  • 1965 - Metropolitain State University of Denver established.
  • 1966 - Denver Botanical Gardens opens.
  • 1976 - Denver Pridefest begins.
  • 1980 - Lucile's Creole Cafe opens.
  • 1982 - 16th Street Mall opens.
  • 1984 - Republic Plaza built.
  • 1988 - Wynkoop Brewing Company opens.
  • 1990 - Denver Convention Center opens.
  • 2005 - Marijuana legalization approved.
  • 2010 - B-cycle bikeshare launched.
  • 2012 - Denver Comic Con begins.
  • 2017 - Denver Women's protest against Trump - 200,000 people attend.
